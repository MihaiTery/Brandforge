# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Service providers, broadly: anyone offering a service who needs to market it on TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts but doesn't have in-house production. This is the lead audience the site speaks to first. The wider circle around them — small brands, online shops, solo creators, consultants, personal projects — is real (named explicitly in llms.txt) but secondary to the service-provider framing.

## Product Purpose

BrandForge is an AI content studio built around two tiers: ReelForge, a 1€ single-reel generator with no account or subscription, and BrandForge full-custom, recurring strategy and video campaigns for brands that want constant content. ReelForge is the low-risk entry point; full-custom is the real business.

Checkout isn't live yet — ReelForge's order flow is fully designed but every CTA currently opens a "coming soon" modal that routes to WhatsApp. Right now, the site's actual job isn't conversion; it's visibility and credibility. It needs to read as a real, professional, already-operating business (not a work-in-progress) while quietly funneling anyone actually interested toward a WhatsApp conversation.

## Positioning

A 1€ AI reel is a no-risk way to see what BrandForge can do, before committing to full-custom content services.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: "Aprinde un reel — 1€" (order a ReelForge reel). Visually and structurally the site's main CTA, though it currently opens the "coming soon" modal rather than a live checkout.
- Secondary CTA: WhatsApp contact ("Hai să vorbim despre brandul tău") — the fallback for anyone not ready for (or blocked by) the primary CTA, and the real conversion path while checkout is offline.
- The line a visitor should remember after 10 seconds: a 1€ AI reel is a no-risk way to see what BrandForge can do.
- Belief ladder (inferred from the page's own structure — the scrollytelling funnel already builds this order; flag if any step is wrong): this is a real, professional operator, not a scam or a test project → AI-generated reels can actually look good enough to post, not just technically functional → starting costs almost nothing and requires no account or subscription → there's real creative direction behind the AI, not raw unsupervised output → if the first reel lands, there's a natural next step into ongoing content.
- Proof on hand: the social showcase already on the page (Florian Molea Instructor, InventorPath, OPS Founder) is the current proof. Nothing additional exists yet.

## Brand Personality

Bold, intense, alive. The molten-forge visual system (obsidian backdrop, WebGL lava shader, ember and smoke motion, "fire" language throughout the copy) is the personality made literal, not decoration on top of a neutral brand. Confident and physical rather than corporate; the voice treats a reel like something forged, not generated.

## Anti-references

Must not read as a cheap freelancer or Fiverr-style gig service, despite the 1€ entry price. The price is meant to land as a low-risk offer and a hook, never as a signal of low quality or a discount-bin service. Production values, copy, and craft should all argue against the price being read as "you get what you pay for."

## Design Principles

- Price the smallest commitment as an invitation, not a discount — 1€ should read as "try the fire," never as "this is cheap work."
- Every step earns the next. The funnel from spark (1€ reel) to full-custom services only works if each stage proves enough credibility to justify asking for more.
- Direction, not just generation. Every surface should keep signaling that human creative direction stands behind the AI — the brand's actual differentiator against "just another AI generator."
- Stay honest about the current stage. Checkout isn't live; copy and CTAs should keep saying "în curând" rather than implying a purchase flow that doesn't yet exist.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA is the bar. No stricter requirement or specific user need beyond that was flagged. This is already the site's practical baseline: verified contrast ratios, visible focus states, and full `prefers-reduced-motion` support are implemented and were audited into place, not aspirational.
